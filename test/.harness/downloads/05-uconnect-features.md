# Uconnect® Connected Services & Features

**Source:** https://www.driveuconnect.com/system-features.html  
**Date Retrieved:** 2026-04-26

## Entertainment Features

### Wi-Fi® Hotspot Service
- Unlimited data plan available
- Connect WiFi PLUS subscription provides fast, reliable 4G
- Connect up to 8 devices simultaneously
- 50-foot connectivity radius
- Wi-Fi app for in-vehicle management

### Wireless Connectivity
- Stream music directly from smartphone to Uconnect® system
- Pair and prioritize up to 10 smartphones
- Multiple user support
- Skip cable connections

### In-Vehicle Alexa Built-In
- Amazon Alexa integration
- Link vehicle to Amazon account
- Access music, news, shopping cart
- Smart home device control (lights, garage doors, etc.)
- Voice-activated entertainment

### Amazon Content & Fire TV
- Mirror favorite Amazon entertainment to vehicle screens
- In-drive movies, shows, music, games
- Available Rear Seat Entertainment
- Fire TV integration

### SiriusXM®
- 3-month trial subscription included
- Ad-free music, talk, sports, comedy, news
- Podcasts available
- Integrated SiriusXM app access

## Navigation & Travel Services

### Uconnect® Navigation
- Full suite of navigation features
- 2D and 3D map technology
- Easy-to-use customizable interface
- Send & Go: Search and send addresses ahead of time
- Pre-journey route planning

### Last Mile Navigation
- Walking directions from parked vehicle to final destination
- Integration with compatible smartphones and wearables
- Return navigation to parking spot
- Seamless synchronization

### Connected Traffic & Travel Services
- Real-time traffic updates
- Alternate route suggestions
- Gas/charging station information and pricing
- Parking options (availability and pricing)
- Traffic camera alerts
- Construction updates
- Weather at destination

### Dynamic Range Mapping
- Analyzes slope and terrain of route
- Accurate range estimation
- Fuel/battery charge calculations
- Refueling/recharging stop suggestions
- Off-road capability monitoring (select vehicles)

### Off-Road Pages (Jeep/Ram)
- Real-time vehicle status monitoring
- Ride height display
- Transfer case status
- Vehicle pitch monitoring
- Select-Terrain® Traction Management System status
- Exclusive to select Ram and Jeep vehicles
