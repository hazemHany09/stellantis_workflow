# 2024 Jeep Wrangler Specifications - Car & Driver

**Source:** https://www.caranddriver.com/jeep/wrangler/specs/2024/  
**Date Retrieved:** 2026-04-26  
**Trim:** Wrangler Sport 4 Door 4x4

## Engine Specifications

### Powertrain
- **Engine Type:** 3.6L V-6 (Regular Unleaded)
- **Displacement:** 3.6 L / 220 cubic inches
- **Horsepower:** 285 @ 6400 RPM
- **Torque:** 260 lb-ft @ 4800 RPM
- **Fuel System:** Sequential MPI
- **Cooling System:** Regular Duty

### Transmission
- **Type:** 6-Speed Manual with Overdrive
- **Gear Ratios:**
  - 1st: 5.13
  - 2nd: 2.63
  - 3rd: 1.53
  - 4th: 1.00
  - 5th: 0.81
  - 6th: 0.72
  - Reverse: 4.49

## Fuel Economy
- **Combined:** 19 mpg
- **City:** 16 mpg
- **Highway:** 22 mpg
- **Fuel Tank Capacity:** 21.5 gallons
- **EPA Range:** City 344 miles / Highway 473 miles

## Drivetrain & Suspension

### Drivetrain
- Four-Wheel Drive (4WD)
- Manual Transfer Case
- Part-Time 4WD System

### Suspension
- **Front:** Leading Link suspension with coil springs
- **Rear:** Trailing Arm suspension with coil springs
- **Anti-Roll Bars:** Front and rear
- **Shock Absorbers:** Gas-pressurized

## Brakes & Wheels

### Braking System
- 4-Wheel Disc Brakes
- 4-Wheel ABS (Anti-Lock Braking System)
- Front rotors: 12.9" vented discs
- Rear rotors: 12.9" vented discs
- Brake Assist and Hill Hold Control
- Brake Actuated Limited Slip Differential

### Wheels & Tires
- **Wheel Size:** 17" x 7.5"
- **Material:** Styled Steel
- **Tire Size:** P245/75SR17
- **Spare:** Full-size steel spare

## Exterior Dimensions

- **Wheelbase:** 118.4 inches
- **Length:** 188.4 inches
- **Width:** 73.9 inches (without mirrors)
- **Height:** 73.6 inches
- **Ground Clearance:** 9.7 inches
- **Track Width:** 62.9 inches (front and rear)

## Interior Dimensions

- **Seating Capacity:** 5 passengers
- **Front Headroom:** 42.6 inches
- **Front Legroom:** 41.2 inches
- **Front Shoulder Room:** 55.8 inches
- **Rear Headroom:** 41.7 inches
- **Rear Legroom:** 38.3 inches
- **Rear Shoulder Room:** 55.8 inches
- **Cargo Space Behind 2nd Row:** 31.7 cubic feet

## Weight & Capacity

- **Curb Weight:** 4,285 lbs
- **Gross Vehicle Weight Rating:** 5,500 lbs
- **Maximum Towing Capacity:** 3,500 lbs
- **Maximum Payload:** 1,215 lbs
- **Tongue Weight (Dead Weight Hitch):** 350 lbs

## Steering & Handling

- **Steering Type:** Re-Circulating Ball
- **Turning Diameter (Curb to Curb):** 38.8 feet

## Electrical

- **Battery:** 700 CCA @ 0°F, Maintenance-Free
- **Alternator Capacity:** 180 amps
- **Power Outlets:** 2 x 12V DC
