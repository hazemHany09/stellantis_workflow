# Jeep Wrangler 4xe Electrification Technology

**Source:** https://www.jeep.com/ev/technology.html  
**Date Retrieved:** 2026-04-26

## 2024 Jeep Wrangler 4xe Plug-In Hybrid Overview

The "4xe" (for-by-electric) designation denotes Jeep's plug-in hybrid electric vehicle (PHEV) platform.

## Powertrain Specifications

### Engine & Motors
- Turbocharged 2.0-Liter Inline-4 Engine: 375 HP
- Dual Electric Motors integrated with transmission
- Combined output: 470 lb-ft of torque
- Dual electric motors provide seamless power delivery

### Battery Pack
- 400-volt 17.3 kWh lithium-ion battery
- Supports plug-in charging
- Enables electric-only driving mode

### Performance
- Electric-only range: Approximately 21-22 miles per full charge
- 49 MPGe (EPA combined rating)
- Seamless gas-electric power integration
- Excellent off-road capability maintained

## Charging Infrastructure

### Home Charging Options

**Level 1 (120-volt)**
- Standard household outlet
- Charging time: 3 days for full charge
- Convenient but slower

**Level 2 (240-volt)**
- Professional home installation required
- Alternating current (AC)
- Power: 10-19 kilowatts
- Charging time: 8-10 hours for full charge
- Free2move Charge Level 2 Charger available
- Qmerit installation partnership available

### Public Charging

**Level 3 (DC Fast Charge)**
- 400+ volts DC fast-charging
- Public charging stations only
- Charging time: 15-60 minutes for full charge
- Free2move Charge app for station discovery

## Regenerative Braking

- Battery charges when decelerating and braking
- Max Regeneration feature remaps accelerator pedal
- More aggressive regeneration at low pedal positions
- Allows vehicle slow-down without brake pedal
- Feature remains selected until manually turned off

## Electric Charging Network Expansion

Starting early 2026:
- Stellantis adopts North American Charging System (NACS)
- Adds 27,500 Tesla Supercharger stalls
- Total network: 40,000+ charging stations
- Integrated charging adapter provided
- Significantly expands fast-charging convenience

## Connected Features (Jeep Connect)

### Remote Vehicle Operations
- Lock and unlock doors remotely
- Flash lights remotely
- Vehicle status monitoring

### Trip Information
- Speed monitoring
- Boundary alerts for family members
- Real-time vehicle location

### Charging Management
- Map of nearby charging stations
- Route planning for long-distance travel
- Charging status monitoring
- Seamless navigation to chargers

## Battery Technology Benefits

- Zero emissions during electric-only driving
- Reduced fuel consumption overall
- Flexibility for city driving (electric) and highway (gas)
- Maintains full off-road capability
- Cutting-edge battery management system
